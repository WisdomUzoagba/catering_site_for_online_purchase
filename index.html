Welcome to Gourmet Delights

Founded by award-winning chef Michael Anderson, Gourmet Delights brings restaurant-quality meals to your home or office. What started as a small personal chef service has grown into one of the most trusted catering companies in the region.

We pride ourselves on using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients to create dishes that are as nutritious as they are delicious. Our team of culinary experts prepares each meal with the same care and attention we would give to our own families.
